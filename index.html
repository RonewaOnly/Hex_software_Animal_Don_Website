About Us

Animal Donations is a non-profit organization committed to providing food, shelter, and medical care to abandoned and abused animals. Our goal is to give them a second chance and connect them with loving homes.

Our Services

Animal Shelter

We offer a safe and nurturing environment for animals to recover and thrive.

Medical Care

Comprehensive medical care for injured and unwell animals is at the heart of our mission.

Adoption Services

Our adoption process connects animals with families who can provide them a loving forever home.
